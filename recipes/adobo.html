Chicken Adobo

Image source: aceofknaves on Flickr

Note: results may vary from the image above!

Description

Adobo! This is an extremely popular dish in the Philippines, with some considering it to be the unofficial national dish. There are several different varieties, but this dish usually involves preparing meat, seafood and vegetables in a marinade of vinegar, garlic and soy sauce to produce a savoury, sweet and tangy meal.

Recipe source: angelaBBf on allrecipes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, quartered and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons of minced garlic
  • 1/3 cup of white vinegar
  • 2/3 cup of low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons of black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook the chicken pieces until golden brown on both sides, then remove.
  3. Stir in the onion and garlic, cook until soft and brown.
  4. Pour in the vinegar and soy sauce, season with garlic powder and black pepper.
  5. Add the browned chicken, increase heat to high and bring to a boil.
  6. After, reduce the heat to medium-low, cover and simmer until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  7. Enjoy!
