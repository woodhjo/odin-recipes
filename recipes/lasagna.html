🇮🇹 Lasagna

Description

A dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef or ground turkey
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 oz each) tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 190°C. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente.
  2. In a large skillet overmedium-high heat, cook the ground beef or turkey, onion, and garlic until the emat is no longer pink. Drain off any excess fat.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried basil, salt, black pepper, and sugar to the skillet. Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups of the shredded mozarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, and chopped parsley.
  5. Spread about 1/3 of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish. Add a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, overlapping them slightly.
  6. Spread half of the cheese mixture over the noodles, then add another layer of noodles.
  7. Repeat with another layer of sauce, noodles, and cheese mixture. Top with a final layer of noodles and the remaining meat sauce.
  8. Sprinkle the remaining 1/2 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of the lasagna.
  9. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the lasagna is heated through.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
