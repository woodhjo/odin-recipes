🇬🇧 Fish and Chips

Description

A dish consisting of fried fish in batter, served with chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound cod or haddock fillets, cut into 4-6 pieces
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup (240 ml) beer (lager or ale)
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) milk
  • 4-6 large potatoes, peeled and cut into thick strips
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Salt, for seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 95°C. Place a wire rack on a baking sheet and set aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Add the beer and milk to the bowl and whisk until the batter is smooth and free of lumps.
  4. In a large heavy-bottomed pot, heat 2-3 inches of vegetable oil to 190°C.
  5. Pat the fish fillets dry with a paper towel and season them lightly with salt and black pepper.
  6. Dip each fish fillet into the batter, making sure it's coated evenly.
  7. Carefully place the battered fish fillets into the hot oil and fry for 4-5 minutes or until the batter is crispy and golden brown. Remove the fish from the oil with a slotted spoon and transfer it to the wire rack on the baking sheet.
  8. While the fish is frying, blanch the potato strips in boiling water for 3-4 minutes, then drain them and pat them dry with paper towels.
  9. Once the fish is done, increase the temperature of the oil to 200°C and fry the potato strips in batches until they are golden and crispy, about 5-7 minutes per batch. Remove the chips from the oil with a slotted spoon and drain them on paper towels.
  10. Season the fish and chips with salt and serve immediately with your favourite condiments. Enjoy!
